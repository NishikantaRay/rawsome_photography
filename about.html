Hey there! My name is Sabyasachi Mitra and I've been pursuing photography since 2017. I hail from Odisha and currently studying at Silicon Institute Of Technology Bhubaneswar. My journey as a Photographer was not easy , When i started off i didn't know what genre to start with. With time i honed my skills towards capturing humans and their emotions and have been fortunate enough to create stunning portraits out of the same. They say learning never stops and that's the same for me since Day 1 i have been saying this one thing to myself that " You need to be versatile " and i will be , continuing to forge new paths and a new tommorow. Being me , Being Rawsome.
